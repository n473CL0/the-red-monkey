The warmer weather has arrived and so have our spring ales. We've just tapped three new casks from local breweries, each one brewed with the season in mind.

First up is the **Hopsworth Spring Pale** from Millbrook Brewery — a light, floral ale with a clean finish. Perfect for a sunny afternoon in the beer garden.

Next is **Amber Drift** from Two Foxes Brewing Co. This one's a little more complex: toffee notes up front, a hint of citrus, and a dry finish that keeps you coming back.

Finally, we've got a limited run of **Meadow Stout** from the folks at Irongate. Yes, a stout in spring — but trust us on this one. It's lighter than it looks and pairs brilliantly with our new seasonal menu.

All three are on now. Come in and try them before they're gone.
