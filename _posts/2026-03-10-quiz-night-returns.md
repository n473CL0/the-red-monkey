After a short break, our weekly quiz night is back — every Thursday from 8pm.

Teams of up to six. Entry is £2 per person, with all proceeds going to the prize pot. First place takes home the cash, second gets a round on us.

Quizmaster duties are back in the capable hands of Dave, who promises fewer trick questions this time around. (He's lying.)

No booking required — just show up, grab a pint, and find a table. We recommend arriving by 7:30 to get settled before the first round.

See you Thursday.
