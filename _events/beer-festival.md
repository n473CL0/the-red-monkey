Our annual Summer Beer Festival is back for 2026.

Over the weekend we'll be pouring over 30 ales, lagers, and ciders from breweries across the region. This year we're also featuring a dedicated cider tent in the beer garden, with producers from three counties.

**Saturday 7 June**
- 12:00 — Festival opens
- 14:00 — Tasting session: Pale ales and IPAs
- 19:00 — Live music from The Hopsworth Collective

**Sunday 8 June**
- 12:00 — Festival opens
- 14:00 — Tasting session: Dark ales and stouts
- 17:00 — Festival closes

Entry is free. Tasting glasses available at the bar for £3 (includes first pour).

All are welcome. See you there.
