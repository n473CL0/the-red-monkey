We're delighted to welcome **The Barrel Rollers** back to The Red Monkey for a live set on Saturday 17 May.

The Barrel Rollers play a mix of folk, blues, and original material — the kind of music that sounds best in a pub with a pint in hand. They've been playing together for over a decade and always put on a great show.

Doors open at 7pm. Music starts at 8pm. No ticket required — just come along.

The bar will be fully stocked, and our kitchen will be serving until 9:30pm.
