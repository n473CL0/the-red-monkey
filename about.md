## About

The Red Monkey has been a fixture of Hopsworth since 1987. What started as a small corner boozer has grown into a proper community pub — the kind of place where regulars know each other's names and strangers don't stay strangers for long.

We serve a rotating selection of cask ales, craft lagers, and the occasional experimental brew from local microbreweries. Our kitchen turns out honest pub food: pies, burgers, and a Sunday roast that people travel for.

### Our Philosophy

We believe a good pub is more than just a place to drink. It's a living room for the neighbourhood — somewhere to celebrate, commiserate, debate, and decompress. We keep things simple: good beer, good food, good company.

### The Team

The pub is run by the Hartley family, who took over from the original owners in 2003. Head chef Marcus has been behind the pass for twelve years. Behind the bar you'll find a rotating crew of locals who know their ales.
